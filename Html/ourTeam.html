Our Team

Meet our Founders

  • Brisa Maneechotesuwan

    I'm a junior in high school with a passion for technology. I want to bridge the opportunity gap in STEM accessibility and offer underserved youth more opportunities to pursue STEM. My vision for GenSTEM is to create a platform and network where this goal becomes tangible.

    Having always been a STEM enthusiast I recognize its beauty and importance, it's a foundation for driven innovation and something that is seamlessly integrated into our daily lives! Therefore I hope that GenSTEM can help highlight and amplify this notion of STEM for all and its immense significance in our society.

    Beyond these interests, I enjoy playing piano covers, watching K-dramas and working on jigsaw puzzles !

  • Malissa Saengtubtim

    The reason that I started GenSTEM was because I'd like to see how my actions can make an impact on the wider community.

    I enjoy subjects like Chemistry, Biology and Physics, therefore this a chance for me to express my passion and also increase opportunities for underprivileged children to access resources in these areas.

    Other than sciences, I like music as well, as I compose songs and play the piano in my free time.

    I'm really excited to be working on this project with our team and host workshops which will hopefully contribute to reducing the gap in educational inequality.

    malissas888@gmail.com

  • Praeploy Nakmai

    As the co-founders of GenSTEM, we believed in our potential to make real impacts on the community and would love to see how far our capabilities can go. We have chosen to specifically tackle the fourth global goal: Quality Education. This is because growing up as an individual who’s provided with perfect educational opportunities made me realised that not all kids and teenagers are receiving quality education as I have.

    I am also very much intrigued in areas of sciences; GenSTEM is the perfect channel where I would get to make a difference for our community through what I am personally passionate about.
